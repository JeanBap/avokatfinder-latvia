Apliecinājums par Tiesību Pārreģistrāciju

An apliecinājums par tiesību pārreģistrāciju is the Latvian Land Book confirmation of rights transfer — the official document issued by the Zemesgrāmata confirming that ownership of a property has been successfully registered in the buyer's name.

After the Zemesgrāmata processes a pirkuma līgums and registers the transfer of ownership, it issues an apliecinājums par tiesību pārreģistrāciju — the official confirmation that the new owner's rights have been entered in the Land Book. This document is the buyer's definitive proof of ownership.

The apliecinājums contains the property's cadastral designation, the new owner's name and identification number, the date of registration, and any encumbrances registered simultaneously. It replaces the previous owner's entry and creates the buyer's registered title.

Buyers should store the apliecinājums securely — it is the equivalent of a title deed in other legal systems. However, unlike paper title deeds in common law countries, the Latvian system is register-based, meaning the Zemesgrāmata extract is equally authoritative as evidence of ownership.

For mortgage purposes, banks accept a fresh Zemesgrāmata extract (showing the hipotēka registered in the lender's favour) as confirmation of both ownership and security. The apliecinājums alone is not sufficient for the bank — they need the registered charge as well.

Common Mistake: Assuming that receiving the apliecinājums means all encumbrances have been cleared. The document confirms your ownership and lists encumbrances registered at that moment — always pull a fresh Zemesgrāmata extract alongside it to verify the complete picture.
Expert Tip: File for the apliecinājums immediately after the Zemesgrāmata notifies you of completed registration. Don't wait — you need this document to confirm your title is clean before releasing any retention from the purchase price.

In the event of any dispute over ownership, the Zemesgrāmata registration is the definitive record. The apliecinājums serves as a reference document but the live register extract takes precedence.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an apliecinājums in Latvian property law?

An apliecinājums par tiesību pārreģistrāciju is the official Zemesgrāmata confirmation that ownership of a property has been registered in the new owner's name. It serves as the buyer's proof of registered title in Latvia.

How long after signing does the Latvian Zemesgrāmata issue the apliecinājums?

Standard processing takes five to seven working days. Expedited registration (one working day) is available for a higher fee. The apliecinājums is issued once the registration is complete.

Is the apliecinājums equivalent to a title deed in Latvia?

Yes — the apliecinājums confirms registered ownership, making it functionally equivalent to a title deed. However, as Latvia uses a register-based system, a live Zemesgrāmata extract is equally authoritative and is more commonly relied upon in practice.
